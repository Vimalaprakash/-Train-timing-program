UTS app

UTS-Unreserved Train Tickets. It is an Indian Railways official android mobile ticketing app to book unreserved train tickets. This app is working for both Android and Iphone also. For Android users go to the play store and type uts and install it. For Iphone users scan the QR-code and install it. scan the QR-code and install to your device!
